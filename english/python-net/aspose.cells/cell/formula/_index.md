## formula property


Gets or sets a formula of the [Cell](/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cell).
### Remarks 


A formula string always begins with an equal sign (=). 
And please always use comma(,) as parameters delimiter, such as "=SUM(A1, E1, H2)".

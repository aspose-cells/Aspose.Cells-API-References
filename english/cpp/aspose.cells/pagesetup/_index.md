## Examples


```cpp
Aspose::Cells::Startup();
Workbook workbook;

WorksheetCollection sheets = workbook.GetWorksheets();

//Add a worksheet
sheets.Add();
Worksheet sheet = sheets.Get(1);
PageSetup pageSetup = sheet.GetPageSetup();
pageSetup.SetPrintArea(u"D1:K13");
Aspose::Cells::Cleanup();
```
